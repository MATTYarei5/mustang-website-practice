Ford Mustang

History and Evolution

The Ford Mustang, an iconic American muscle car, has a rich history dating back to its debut in 1964. Renowned for its sleek design and powerful performance, the Mustang has become a symbol of automotive excellence. Over the years, it has undergone various transformations, adapting to new technologies while retaining its classic appeal.
